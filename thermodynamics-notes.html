🔥 Thermodynamics 🌕

Complete Thermodynamics Notes (NEET)

1. Basic Concepts

Concepts
  • Thermodynamics deals with macroscopic systems.
  • Focus on heat, work and energy.

Heat (Q)

  • Energy in transit
  • Flows due to temperature difference
  • Not stored energy

Work (W)

Energy transfer due to force/displacement.

Internal Energy (U)

Total microscopic energy (KE + PE of molecules)

QuantityType
HeatPath function
WorkPath function
Internal EnergyState function

2. Zeroth Law

Explanation

If A is in equilibrium with B and B with C → A with C.

Defines temperature

Trap: Students confuse with energy ❌

3. First Law

Formula + Meaning

Q = ΔU + W

CaseSign
Heat supplied+Q
Work by system+W
Work on system-W

Energy is conserved.

4. Thermodynamic Processes

All Processes

Isothermal

  • T constant
  • ΔU = 0
  • Q = W

Adiabatic

  • Q = 0
  • ΔU = -W
  • Expansion → cooling
  • Compression → heating

Isochoric

  • Volume constant
  • W = 0
  • Q = ΔU

Isobaric

  • Pressure constant
  • W = PΔV

5. Heat Capacity

Details

C = Q / ΔT

  • Cp → constant pressure
  • Cv → constant volume

Cp > Cv

6. Second Law

Statements

Kelvin-Planck

100% heat → work impossible

Clausius

Heat flows hot → cold

Reverse needs work

7. Reversible vs Irreversible

Comparison
TypeFeatures

IrreversibleReal, energy loss

8. Carnot Engine

Concept + Formula

Ideal engine with max efficiency

η = 1 - (T₂ / T₁)

  • Temperature must be in Kelvin
  • Independent of gas
  • Efficiency less than 100%

9. NEET Traps

Must Revise
  • Heat ≠ internal energy
  • ΔU = 0 → isothermal
  • Q = 0 → adiabatic
  • W = 0 → isochoric
  • Cp > Cv
  • 100% efficiency impossible
  • Heat flows hot → cold
  • Reversible = infinitely slow
  • Adiabatic expansion cools gas

10. Exam Strategy

How to Solve Questions
